Security Measures

We offer two-factor authentication to add an extra layer of security to your account. This ensures that only you can access your account.

Fraud Detection

Our advanced fraud detection system monitors transactions in real-time to identify and prevent fraudulent activities.

We conduct regular security audits and assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities in our system.
