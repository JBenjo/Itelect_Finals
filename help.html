Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a Cash-E account?

To create a Cash-E account, download our mobile app from the App Store or Google Play, or visit our website and click on 'Register'. Follow the prompts to enter your personal information, verify your email and mobile number, and set up your security credentials.

How do I add money to my Cash-E wallet?

You can add money to your Cash-E wallet through bank transfers, credit/debit cards, or by visiting our partner establishments for over-the-counter deposits. In the app, go to 'Add Cash' and follow the instructions for your preferred method.

Is there a fee for sending money?

Cash-E to Cash-E transfers are free. Transfers to bank accounts or other e-wallets may incur a small fee depending on the amount and destination. You can view our complete fee schedule in the app or on our website.

What should I do if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, tap on 'Forgot Password' on the login screen. We'll send a password reset link to your registered email address. Follow the instructions to create a new password.

How do I increase my transaction limits?

To increase your transaction limits, you need to complete the advanced verification process. Go to Account Settings > Verification and follow the instructions to submit the required documents. Once verified, your limits will be automatically increased.
